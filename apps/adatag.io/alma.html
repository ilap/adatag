  • When ADA Handle first launched, all $handles were minted on CIP-25. All new $handles are now being minted on CIP-68. CIP-68 is what enables a $handle to be personalized. CIP-68 also enables subHandle minting. A CIP-25 $handle can be upgraded at anytime, by going to handle.me . It costs 4 ADA to upgrade your $handle, this is to cover the blockchain costs of burning the CIP-25 $handle and reminting it on CIP-68, all in 1 transaction.

  • Personalization is a feature that allows a $handle to be customized. When accessing handle.me you are able to Personalize your $handle by adding a PFP, a background image, add a QR code, add social media information and adding a shadow or a ribbon to the text of your $handle. Other features are also available, such as: changing the background and border color of your $handle.

  • ADA Handle charges a flat-fee of 10 ADA every time a $handle is personalized. Additional costs may be added, depending on how much background vendors charge for the custom ADA Handle backgrounds they developed. As the price of ADA appreciates, we will lower this cost, to keep it at a fair value that allows us to remain monetized to continue to build, while incentivizing the community to continue to use Personalization features.
